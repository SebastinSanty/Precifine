Who we are ?

Leaders of LED Manufacturing Industry. Pioneers in this field

Precifine Products Private Limited was started by two Engineers in 1987 to produce electrical items of world class standard. This team pioneered the development of cost effective indicating lamps with LED in India. The result is PRECIFINE range of LED Indication lamps, which is synonymous with low power consumption and assured reliability. Precifine Products Private Limited has provided cost effective solutions for the core sector industries in terms of “Totally Maintenance Free Indication Lamps with very Low Power Consumption”. Our products are widely used by Refineries, Nuclear Power Plants, Thermal Power Plants Petrochemicals, Cement Plants, Fertilizer Companies etc. Timely upgraded specifications, Consistent Quality and prompt delivery give our valued customers the confidence to place their requirements with us repeatedly. Strong emphasis on R&D and continuous up gradation of the product range is our motto we also undertake the development of special lamps as per customer specifications.
